# 🌊 TidalDrift Press Kit

## Overview

**TidalDrift** is a unified control center for managing your personal Mac ecosystem. It automatically discovers and connects all the Macs on your home or office network, providing instant one-click access to screen sharing, file transfers, SSH terminals, and more.

### The Name

The name pays homage to **Pacific Rim** (2013), where Jaeger pilots undergo **"The Drift"**—a neural bridge that links their minds together, allowing two people to move as one to control a giant machine.

TidalDrift does the same for your Macs:

> *"Two pilots, one machine. Many Macs, one network."*

- **Tidal** — The constant, rhythmic flow of data between your machines, like the ocean's tide
- **Drift** — The seamless synchronization, the mind-meld between your devices

When you copy text on your iMac and paste it on your MacBook, that's the Drift.  
When your machines appear automatically without configuration, that's the Drift.  
When you click once to take control of another Mac, that's the Drift.

**Your Macs move as one.**

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **🔍 Auto-Discovery** | Uses Bonjour/mDNS to find all Macs automatically—no IP addresses to configure |
| **🖥️ Screen Sharing** | One-click VNC connections to control any Mac remotely |
| **📁 File Sharing** | Quick access to SMB/AFP shares with secure credential storage |
| **🔌 SSH Terminal** | Instant SSH sessions that open right in Terminal.app |
| **📋 Clipboard Sync** | Share clipboard content across all your Macs in real-time |
| **📤 TidalDrop** | Drag-and-drop file transfers between TidalDrift instances |
| **⚡ Wake-on-LAN** | Wake sleeping Macs remotely with magic packets |
| **🔒 Guided Setup** | One-click configuration of Screen Sharing, File Sharing, SSH, and Firewall |

### Full Description

TidalDrift turns your collection of Macs into a unified system.

Whether you have a home office with multiple machines, a small creative studio, or just love having a Mac mini server in the closet—TidalDrift brings them all together under one beautiful dashboard.

**🔍 Automatic Discovery**
No more memorizing IP addresses. TidalDrift finds every Mac on your network automatically and keeps track of which are online.

**🖥️ One-Click Connections**
Screen sharing, file access, SSH—all one click away. Connect to any Mac instantly without digging through system preferences.

**📋 Unified Clipboard**
Copy on your iMac. Paste on your MacBook. Your clipboard flows between all your Macs in real-time.

**📤 TidalDrop**
Drag and drop files between Macs. No AirDrop flakiness. Just fast, direct peer-to-peer transfers.

**⚡ Wake-on-LAN**
Your Mac mini is sleeping? Wake it remotely with a single click.

**🔒 Secure by Design**
All traffic stays on your local network. No cloud. No accounts. No subscriptions. Your network, your control.

**✨ Guided Setup**
TidalDrift walks you through enabling Screen Sharing, File Sharing, SSH, and configuring your firewall—with one-click toggles that do the work for you.

## Screenshots Guide

To capture App Store screenshots, run the app and take screenshots at **1280x800** resolution.

### Required Screenshots (Add to PressKit/Screenshots/ folder)

|---|----------|-------------|----------------|

### Screenshot Tips

1. **Use a clean desktop** - Hide other apps
2. **Show realistic data** - Use real device names, not "Test Mac"
3. **Highlight the peer feature** - Show at least one TidalDrift peer (red highlight)
4. **macOS appearance** - Use system default (Auto) for light/dark balance
